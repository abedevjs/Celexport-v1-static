OUR TERMS

PACKAGING TYPE OF SHIPMENT METHOD OF DISPATCH INCOTERMS PAYMENT TERM ARBITRATION

Properly packed ensured to maintain the quality

The packaging comes with jumbo bags or super sacks made of sisal, henequen, jute, burlap or similar woven material. Depending on any special requirements, all forms of packaging conform to food grade packaging standards at the country of destination. It will be correctly covered with plastic material, with cardboard or sufficiently strong Kraft paper on top of it.
However, shipping in bulk with correct liners and procedures also prevents decay, weight losses due to handling, condensation and baggy smells during the voyage which can take up to 21 days. Being able to supply coffee in bulk is a definite advantage with cost savings for both shipper and receiver.

Safety and reliability above all

Rigid roof, side walls, and floor. A general purpose container is enclosed, watertight, weatherproof and suitable for dry goods. For Full Container Load (FCL), 19-23 tons in bulk or 19-21 tons in bag delivery depending on bean size are fully adequate.
As for Less than Container Load (LCL), we carefully select the right unit along with the recommendation of the shipping company about certain quality control such as cleaning, sanitary, odour control and any other regular inspection.

Planned, and navigated to move smoothly

To save cost efficiently, we deliver our bulk sized commodity by sea via freight container. Indonesia traversed by four international straits which strategically placed us ahead to serve our customer around the world.
Similarly with retail and sample requests. To save time with air transport, we promptly capable to meet our customer demand. Evidently e-commerce and any retailer business have been grown rapidly in Indonesia.

Freight, insurance, handling, and any additional cost

Free On Board (FOB), you determine from which point the cost and responsibilities are transferred. You have much more control over the freight costs and the freight itself, your freight forwarder can help you find a fair freight rate as well.
CIF (Cost, Insurance and Freight), we take responsibility of the shipping arrangements, freight cost, and insurance. Also for CFR (Cost and Freight), we still arrange for everything only this time without insurance cover.

Based on mutual trust, safety and secure

We believed to establish our mutual trust and safety. Selecting Letter of Credit with irrevocable validity and immediate reimbursement only after evaluating all the shipping documents are completed by sight, is the best choice for both applicant and beneficiary.
Advanced payment via Telegraphic Transfer also applicable by aggreement of both parties. Normally we expect advance payment if demands are excessive, as well as if this terms are acceptable and authorized by buyer’s country, as it occurred along with any special request by the buyer.
